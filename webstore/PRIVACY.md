# Glossa — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-07-16  
**Developer:** Tii  
**Extension:** Glossa (browser extension for Chrome / Brave)

## Summary

Glossa does not sell your data. Glossa does not run its own translation servers.

## What data is processed?

When you hover a word or select text, Glossa sends **only that text** to **Google Translate** public API endpoints so a translation can be returned.

## What is stored locally?

- **Settings** (target language, hover delay, on/off) in your browser via `chrome.storage.sync`
- **Translation cache** (recent lookups) in the extension background script memory — not sent to Tii

## What we do not collect

- No account required
- No analytics by Tii
- No contact information
- No browsing history uploaded to Tii

## Third parties

**Google Translate** processes the text you request to translate, under Google's privacy policy.
